In the context of this project, 4 users will use the platform: employer, student/candidate, headhunter and admin.

##Employer

The employer will be able to create and manage job postings (view issue #10). The employer will also be able to create and modify their profile (issue to be created)

##Student/Candidate

The student/candidate will be able to create and modify their profile, which will include the possibility to upload a resume (view issue #12).

##Headhunter

The headhunter will be able to view all the candidates and direct candidates to employers (issues to be created). The headhunter should also be able to sort by specific skills/requirements.

##Admin

The administrator will be able to manage all accounts of the platform (view issue 14).
